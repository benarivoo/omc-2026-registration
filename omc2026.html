About the Conference

Come thirsty.
Leave overflowing.

Jesus speaks to a world marked by uncertainty, hopelessness, and lack of direction. His invitation is open to all: come, drink, and be filled. The promise doesn't end there — rivers flow outward. What God pours in, He intends to pour out through us.

01

The Invitation

No exceptions. No prerequisites. Just come as you are — He alone fills what nothing else can touch.

02

Overflow in Any Season

Your joy and fullness flow from a Source unshaken by hardship, loss, or uncertainty.

03

Give and Let God

Stop holding back. God turns your little into more than enough when you let Him overflow through you.

FAQ

The Overseas Ministry Conference (OMC) is FICA's annual gathering for Indonesian Christians across America — a time to be spiritually renewed, equipped for ministry, and connected in community. The 2026 theme is Filled to Overflow, anchored in John 7:37–38.
OMC is open to all members of FICA churches and anyone seeking spiritual growth, fellowship, and a deeper encounter with God. Whether you're a church leader, young adult, or first-time attendee — all are welcome.
Registration details will be announced shortly. Visit omc.fica.org or contact your local FICA chapter for updates.
Registration pricing will be released with the full conference announcement. Early bird pricing will be available for a limited time.
FICA is committed to making OMC accessible. Please contact your local chapter or reach out through omc.fica.org for information on financial assistance options.
